CROWDS

Super Crowds inc. is a creative firm located in Tokyo, where unique talent with various skills gather to create.

Teamwork is a teamwork that enables individuals to make outputs beyond the imagination of individuals by multiplying different people, different thoughts, different means, utilizing the personality and skill of each person.

As a "Super" group rather than just a group (Crowds), with the aim of making purely good things and experiences, without being bound by the title, as well as internal members as well as "Super" people outside the company are involved, every day I am challenging creative.

OUR CLIENTS

Partners with which we work together range from start-ups to large companies. What is in common is that they are people trying to start or change something. We support their challenge by design while communicating directly with clients as much as possible.
