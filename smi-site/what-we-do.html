The bulk of SMI Contracting’s business comes in commercial projects for the renovation of office spaces that create today’s top work environments. Overseeing all the interior work, SMI project managers work with clients from start to finish to establish a transparent process while providing a team capable of covering all your needs, from plumbing to interior decorating to bookkeeping.

SMI’s keen focus on the planning phase not only prevents the “horror stories” common in the construction business, it also helps clients see past the blueprint sketches to understand every aspect of every space. Project managers collaborate with architects and designers to present the customers with a clear vision for the project to ensure it is completed on budget, on time and to make their dreams a reality.

Covering every aspect of construction projects from beginning to end is a point of pride at SMI. It starts with a solid foundation and builds all the way up to finishing flourishes such as a wallpaper and tiling. This comprehensive approach applies to large- scale commercial renovations to building additions for homeowners.
